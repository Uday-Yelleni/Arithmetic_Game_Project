Game Rules and How to Play:

Welcome to the Arithmetic Game! The goal is to practice basic arithmetic operations and improve your math skills. Here’s how to play:

  • Number of Questions: You can choose how many questions you'd like to answer. The more questions, the more challenging the game becomes!
  • Choose Operations: Select the operations you want to practice. You can choose from Addition, Subtraction, Multiplication, and Division.
  • Operand Range: Set the minimum and maximum digits for the operands in the questions. This will affect the difficulty of the math problems.
  • Timer Option: You can opt for a timer to add an extra challenge. If you enable the timer, you’ll have to answer within the time limit you set in seconds.
  • Answering the Questions: Once the game starts, you will be given a set of math problems based on the operations you selected. Enter your answers and click 'Submit' to check your response.
  • Feedback: After each question, you’ll receive feedback on whether your answer is correct or incorrect. At the end of the game, you’ll see a summary of your performance including correct answers, incorrect answers, and your overall score.

Are you ready to test your math skills? Go ahead and click "Game Settings" to get started!
